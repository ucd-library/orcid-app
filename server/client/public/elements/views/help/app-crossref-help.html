How to Auto-Update Your Publication Information

Enabling the CrossRef Auto-Update feature is a crucial step to optimizing your ORCID record.
If you...
  • enable the CrossRef Auto-Update feature
  • submit your ORCID iD with publications
...then your ORCID record will be automatically updated every time you publish for all publishers that require/allow you to submit your ORCID iD when submitting a manuscript, turning your ORCID record into a self-updating CV.
Make sure you submit your iD every time you publish and turn on CrossRef Auto-Update when you receive an e-mail from CrossRef asking permission to post to your record to take full advantage of the auto-updating functionality.
